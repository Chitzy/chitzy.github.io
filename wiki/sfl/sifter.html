THE SIFTER

The Sifter is the most useful machine, allowing the users to sift materials and get new resources. It has a large amout of output materials, but it should be noted that the Sifter has a 3 second cooldown.

You can simply throw items on top of it to sift them, or place them inside the Sifter with a hopper (the Sifter allows automation).

Ingredients: 2x Cobblestone, 32x Sooth Stone, 16x Stone Slabs, 16x Charcoal, 1x Wooden Trapdoor

SIFTER AUTOMATION


As stated above, the sifter can be automated and iteracts with redstone. A redstone signal will pause the Sifter and hoppers will place items inside the Sifter. Note that the Sifter will still have a 3 second cooldown even though the items are pushed inside earlier with a hopper. The picture bellow describes a simple Sifter settup.
