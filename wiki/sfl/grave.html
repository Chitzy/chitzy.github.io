THE GRAVE


The Grave is a non craftable block that spawns on a player's death to store its inventory. When broken it will drop all the items on the ground. The Grave has its owner's name written on it.

Note that the Grave will only spawn IF there is an air block where the player died, and the player had atleast 1 Item in its inventory on death.
