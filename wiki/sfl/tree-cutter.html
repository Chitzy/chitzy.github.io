THE TREE CUTTER

The Tree Cutter is used for automatically breaking wood, most notably for automating a tree farm. The Tree Cutter has a 10 block area of effect beneath it.

Simply place it down, and a visual indicator of the area will apear. It works for all types of logs, and the resources will be teleported above the Tree Cutter, allowing it to be connected to a hopper for full automation.

Ingredients: 2x Hopper, 1x Ender Pearl, 16x Iron Block, 1x Redstone Dust, 2x Wood Block, 1x Diamond Axe
