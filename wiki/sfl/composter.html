THE COMPOSTER

The Composter is used to create dirt from leaves. The dirt can be then sent to a Sifter for processing or placed in the world.

The composter has 2 ports: a BLUE one and a GREENONE. A filled cauldron needs to be placed at the blue port, and leaves need to be thrown at the green port. You can either throw items yourself or place a dropper to drop them automatically, allowing full automation.

Ingredients: 4x Iron Block, 16x Redstone Dust, 1x Cauldron, 1x Iron Trapdoor, 1x Iron Bars
