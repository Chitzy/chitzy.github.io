ADVANCED CRAFTING TABLE

The Advanced Crafting Table is used for all custom crafting recipes. Initially one is given to the player in the starting chest, but players can make more.

Ingredients: 2x Cobblestone, 2x Redstone Dust, 3x Iron Block, 1x Dropper, 1x Daylight Sensor.

HOW TO USE


To use the Advanced Crafting Table follow the following 3 steps:

STEP 1 - place the required ingredients inside
STEP 2- right click the control panel (on top of the Table)
STEP 3- if successful the Table will give visual and audio feedback
