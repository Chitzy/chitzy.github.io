THE END PORTAL

The End Portal is a alternative for the Vanilla End Portal so that players can travel to the End and fight the Dragon. To use it players must throw an enderpearl on it, after they placed it down on the ground. They will then be teleported in the End dimension.

The End is not altered (from its vanilla form), but using the Rocket Boots for reaching the Crystals can be very beneficial.

Ingredients: 4x End Crystal, 8x Iron Block, 8x Diamond Block, 1x Eye of Ender
