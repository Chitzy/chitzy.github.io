SKY FACTORY LITE
Experience a custom modded skyblock, in vanilla.
INFORMATIONS
Ever wanted to enjoy the advantages of modded minecraft without altering your game? Sky Factory Lite is a vanilla map, insipred by Sky Factory 2.5, which tries to offer the automation and feeling of a modded skyblock map, encouraging user creativity and resource management.

The map is both singleplayer and multiplayer compatible.
TAGS: Minecraft, Automation, SkyBlock, SkyFactory, Mods, Vanilla Mods, New Content
NO MODS

Completely powered by vanilla redstone and command blocks, no 3rd party mods or resource packs are required!
CRAFTING

Populated with lots of custom machines and crafting recipes, almost all blocks are obtainable.
AUTOMATE

The custom addons are created so that they allow users to automate them for faster and efficient farming.
